7. What to do in an event of burn?

  • Try to pour warm water over the burn

8. What is the main aim when someone is bleeding?

9. What to do if something is embedded in the wound?

  • Try to get it out
  • Do not touch it
  • Bandage the wound firmly but prevent putting pressure on the object
  • Provide CPR

10. What to do if something is not embedded in the wound?

  • Apply an maintain pressure to the wound
  • Use clean dressing to bandage the wound
  • Raise the limb to decrease flow of blood (if the limb is not fractured)
  • All of the above
